ABOUT ME

I am currently a data scientist at C.H. Robinson, a third party logistics provider solving supply chain problems from the simple to the most complex.

During my time at CHRW, I have had the opportunity to work as a data analyst, senior data analyst, and now data scientist. I have a passion for leveraging cutting-edge technology and creating data products that enable our team to better serve our clients.

As a data scientist, I am primarily focused on pricing analysis and solution design, as well as data analytics and robotic process automation (RPA). My day-to-day primarily consists of API and front-end application development, creation and management of automated data pipelines, development and deployment of process automation bots, and ad hoc exploratory data analysis.

In my previous roles I specialized in creating a suite of dashboards and reporting tools that provided users insight into volume and revenue trends, yield management, and operational metrics and KPIs.

In my free time I enjoy reading, exploring new technology, spending time with family, and sneaking in a round of golf whenever possible ;)
